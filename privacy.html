Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Pixlgrove is a small web design studio. We keep this simple: we only collect what we need to reply to you, and we don't sell your information to anyone. This page explains what we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect

The only personal information we collect is what you choose to send through our contact form, which is powered by Tally. That includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The message and project details you write to us

We don't run advertising trackers or collect anything beyond what you submit.

How we use it

We use the details you send only to reply to your enquiry, discuss your project, and provide the services you ask about. That's it — we won't add you to marketing lists you didn't sign up for.

We don't sell your data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your details stay between you and us, apart from the trusted services we use to run the site and receive your messages (such as our form provider and hosting platform).

Hosting and infrastructure

This website is hosted and served through Netlify and Cloudflare. As part of delivering the site, these providers may process standard technical data such as your IP address and browser type to keep the site secure and online. They process this on our behalf under their own security and privacy practices.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, at any time. Just email us and we'll take care of it.

Governing law

This policy and the handling of your information are governed by the laws of India, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and its associated rules.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email us at hello@pixlgrove.com and we'll get back to you.
